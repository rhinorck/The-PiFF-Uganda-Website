Volunteer

The PiFF Uganda is built on the strength of volunteers who give their time and expertise to help run PiFF projects and activities.

Management is undertaken by a volunteer board comprising of:-

  • Chairperson
  • Secretary
  • Treasurer
  • Co-ordinator
  • Spokesperson
Deputies are appointed by the board from the available volunteers.

The management board reports periodically to a Board of Trustees that is also comprised of volunteers picked for their exemplary service to their communities, experience and standing in society.

You can volunteer to perform a task, take part in an activity or contribute to a project.
