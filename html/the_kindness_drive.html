The Kindness Drive

The PiFF Uganda's mission is to spread the idea of building a better world together through practising kindness in our lives, everyday.

What is kindness? We believe the idea of goodwill and generosity towards life and our fellow human beings is as indescribable as the nature of life itself and so we will leave the definitions to the scholars among you. What we do know is that the kind act is self-evident; it builds first one who performs it for it is a generosity of spirit that nourishes both body and mind and fills one's cup of life. Kindness is an internal culture, it begins from within, so first, be kind to yourself.

The Kindness Drive is therefore the primary project of the PiFF Uganda and it involves all volunteers; every project or activity undertaken by the PiFF Uganda is aimed at illustrating the concept of paying it forward.

Some things you can do everyday to pay it forward :-

  • Smile. A little bit more.
  • Be polite. Say please and thank you.
  • Hold the door open for someone.
  • Keep a happiness jar.
  • Hug Someone
  • Give up your place in the queue
  • Share a laugh :-)
  • Volunteer

If you'd like to suggest a kindness drive project, please drop us a line using the contact form.
