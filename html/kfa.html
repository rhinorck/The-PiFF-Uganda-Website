Knowledge For All

We believe education is fundamental to building a better world. In light of this the PiFF Uganda prioritises education oriented projects. Through the Knowledge for All (KFA) initiative, the PiFF Uganda aims to improve access to knowledge and instruction for communities, groups and individuals.

The Knowledge for All initiative has the following specific objectives:-

  • To establish and maintain a fund for the purposes of distributing bursaries and scholarships and funding education oriented projects.
  • Promote innovative education techniques and methodologies.
  • Source, collect and distribute educational materials to individuals, schools and communities.
  • Establish accessible public libraries and learning institutions in communities across Uganda

The PiFF Education Fund

The PiFF Education Fund was setup in 2009 at the inception of the foundation. The fund provides bursaries and scholarships and additionally provides funds for education oriented projects. Between 2009 and 2011 the fund was able to pay tuition for the children of Oasis of Life orphanage in Natete. The fund has also provided tuition for other individuals.

The Kindness Drive is currently fundraising for the Education Fund, specifically for one of the fund's beneficiaries who's currently a senior six student. Isaac is a student at St. John's Wakiso Secondary School where he has been since 2013 when he transferred from Gayaza Academy. He became a beneficiary of the PiFF Education Fund in 2011 after completing his primary school from Sister Miriam Duggan Primary School in Kamwokya, a partner with the PiFF in a Kindness Drive project from 2010. Isaac is an intelligent, disciplined and hardworking student but his mother (a vegetable vendor) is unfortunately unable to afford his tuition. He has been a beneficiary of the fund since 2011.Isaac's academic reports are available on request and a visit can be arranged and would be very welcome.

Isaac's tuition is UGX 500,000/= per term.
